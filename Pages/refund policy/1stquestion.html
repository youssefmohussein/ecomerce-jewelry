What is your return or exchange policy?

About the Return & Exchange Policy Page VITA provides customers with a seamless and elegant experience for returns and exchanges, ensuring they shop with confidence.
It outlines:
Eligibility criteria for returns and exchanges (e.g., condition of the item, timeframe).
Step-by-step instructions on how to initiate a return or exchange.
Exceptions, such as non-returnable items like personalized jewelry or earrings (for hygiene reasons).
Processing times for refunds and exchanges.
Shipping policies, including whether customers need to cover return shipping costs.

Return Policy:
We gladly accept returns in-store or by mail with the original receipt or via electronic receipt lookup, ensuring a seamless experience for you.

Returns by Mail:
Carefully pack your item with the Return Form and securely attach the prepaid shipping label on the outside of the package. If you cannot locate the return form or prepaid label, please contact VITA jewelry customer team for further assistance.
Drop off your package at any shipping location—return shipping is free.
Once we receive your return, we’ll process it promptly and send a confirmation email.
Refunds will be issued to your original payment method within 5 to 7 business days, though shipping fees are non-refundable.

Returns in Store:
Visit your nearest VITA store to return your item.
Simply bring the original packing slip or email invoice along with the item(s) you wish to return.
Refunds will be issued to the original payment method, while PayPal purchases will receive a gift card instead.

Exchange:
For an exchange, simply return your item(s) by mail using the prepaid return label or visit a VITA store, where you can purchase your desired item(s). Exchanges can be made at VITA-branded stores for an item of equal or greater value, subject to availability.
Please note that original shipping costs are non-refundable and are not included in the exchange value.
