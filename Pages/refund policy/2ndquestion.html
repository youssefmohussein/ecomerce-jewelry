What is VITA's exchange policy?

Items in their unused and original condition purchased from VITA Online Store can be exchanged within 14 days of purchase in one of our store locations.
VITA Gift Cards or E-Gift Cards are non-returnable. Promotions, clearances, or sales may have modified terms and conditions.

Exchange in store:
To exchange an item, visit your nearest VITA store, Bring your original packing slip or email invoice along with the item(s) you wish to exchange.
Please note that original shipping costs are not included in the exchange value and are non-refundable.
