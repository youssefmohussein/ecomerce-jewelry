FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What types of gold jewelry do you offer?
We offer a wide range of gold jewelry, including rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings, and custom pieces.
What karats of gold are available?
Our jewelry is available in 10K, 14K, 18K, and 22K gold, in both yellow and white gold options.
How do I take care of my gold jewelry?
To maintain its shine, clean your gold jewelry with a soft cloth, avoid chemicals, and store it separately to prevent scratches.
Do you offer gold jewelry resizing services?
Yes, we offer resizing services for gold rings and other adjustable gold jewelry pieces.
What is your return policy on gold jewelry?
We accept returns within 30 day of purchase, provided the item is in its original condition and has not been customized.
